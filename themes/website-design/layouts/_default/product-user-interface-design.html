What is HFI certified UX analyst?

Human Factor International is a global leader in usability research and training. Our UX team is led by an HFI certified usability analyst rooted in theory and with a 7 year experience in design.

What is unique about your team's skills in design?

We believe two things help us do an awesome job of digital interface design. First is that all our designers have started their journey with front end development and hence the design process accounts for technology constraints.
Second is our leadership teams experience in product management. With over 14 years of building award winning as well as failed products, funded as well as acquired products and consumer as well as Enterprise products; we are well positioned to define and design your product experience.
